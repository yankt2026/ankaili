# SEO Strategy

## Goal

Generate qualified English-language RFQs for oil-immersed transformer temperature controllers, OTI/WTI gauges, replacement instruments, and remote monitoring devices.

## Audience

- Transformer OEM purchasing and design engineers
- Utility and substation maintenance teams
- EPC and switchboard/control-panel integrators
- Transformer accessory distributors
- Retrofit and replacement buyers searching by model

## Keyword pillars

### Commercial category terms

- transformer oil temperature indicator manufacturer
- transformer winding temperature indicator manufacturer
- oil immersed transformer temperature controller
- transformer OTI gauge supplier
- transformer WTI gauge supplier
- transformer oil temperature monitoring system

### Model and replacement terms

- BWR2 winding temperature indicator
- BWR-04 winding temperature controller
- BWR-06 winding temperature indicator
- BWY2 oil temperature indicator
- BWY-802 / BWY-803 oil temperature controller
- BWY-D804AITH transformer thermometer

### Engineering-support terms

- OTI vs WTI transformer
- transformer top oil temperature indicator
- transformer winding temperature simulation
- transformer temperature controller wiring
- M27×2 transformer thermometer
- Pt100 vs 4-20mA transformer temperature indicator
- transformer temperature alarm cooling trip contacts

## Search intent mapping

- Category pages target broad commercial terms.
- Product pages target model, manufacturer, supplier, OEM, replacement, and specification terms.
- Application pages target project use cases and buyer industries.
- Technical resources target comparison, selection, wiring, installation, and troubleshooting intent.
- RFQ pages target quote, price, MOQ, customization, and delivery intent without publishing unsupported pricing.

## Differentiation

- A narrow specialist site, not a general sensor catalog.
- Clear OTI/WTI distinction and honest model-selection guidance.
- Downloadable drawings, wiring diagrams, selection sheets, and test documents.
- RFQ workflow based on engineering parameters, not a generic contact form.
- OEM, private-label, capillary, signal, scale, contact, and enclosure customization explained with limits.

## On-page rules

- One search intent and one H1 per page.
- Unique 30–60 character title and 120–160 character meta description.
- Product pages: aim for 800+ useful English words plus a real specification table.
- Include original photos, detail views, drawings, filenames, width/height, WebP/AVIF derivatives, and precise alt text.
- Put the primary RFQ CTA above the fold and after specifications.
- Avoid copied wording; rewrite from verified engineering data.
- Add visible “Specifications may vary by model” language.

## Structured data

- Home: Organization, WebSite
- Category: CollectionPage, BreadcrumbList
- Product: Product, BreadcrumbList; Offer only when real price/availability data exists
- Technical guide: Article, BreadcrumbList
- Contact/RFQ: ContactPage
- Do not add FAQPage solely for rich-result manipulation.

## Technical targets

- Astro static output, semantic HTML, minimal JavaScript
- LCP below 2.5 s, INP below 200 ms, CLS below 0.1
- Responsive `srcset`, explicit image dimensions, lazy loading below fold
- Canonical URLs, XML sitemap, robots.txt, 404 page, Open Graph images
- Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools
- GA4 plus events: RFQ start, RFQ submit, email click, WhatsApp click, PDF download, drawing download

## Evidence still needed before publication

- Legal company name and export contact details
- Factory photos and actual production/testing processes
- Certificates with scope and validity
- Verified model matrix and current drawings
- MOQ, lead time ranges, warranty, packaging, and Incoterms
- Genuine application cases and shipment destinations
