# Competitor Snapshot

Research date: 2026-07-15. This is a directional search snapshot, not a traffic or backlink audit.

## Observed competitors

1. **Guanshan / GS Instruments** — strong BWR-04 model page with operating principle and technical values; opportunity: outperform with clearer model comparison, drawings, RFQ fields, and polished English.
2. **ZPro / Dalian Zhonghe** — tightly focused transformer monitoring catalog covering BWY/BWR families and useful manufacturer credibility; closest topical competitor.
3. **Hangzhou Giantway** — targets WTI product and replacement queries with specification-led pages; opportunity: better documentation, imagery, and application content.
4. **Engelec Power** — strong integrated oil-level and oil-temperature product positioning for distribution transformers; relevant to multi-parameter devices.
5. **Hitachi Energy** — high-authority transformer accessory brand and trust benchmark; compete on customization responsiveness and focused long-tail documentation rather than brand authority.

## SERP lessons

- Model codes and familiar abbreviations (OTI, WTI, BWR, BWY) matter strongly.
- Buyers expect technical ranges, switch ratings, outputs, bulb/thread dimensions, and drawings.
- “Manufacturer,” “supplier,” “OEM,” “replacement,” and “datasheet” modifiers should be answered with evidence.
- An integrated oil-level/temperature product is a separate intent from a conventional oil temperature indicator; do not merge them.

## Content gaps to exploit

- A rigorous OTI vs WTI comparison with selection flowchart
- BWR/BWY cross-reference and replacement checklist

- Cooling/alarm/trip set-point explanation
- Thermometer pocket, bulb, M27×2 thread, and capillary installation guide
