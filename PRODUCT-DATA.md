- Common models: BWR2-06AJ(TH), BWR2-05J(TH), BWR2-04AJ(TH), BWR2-04J(TH)
- Ambient: -40°C to +65°C
- Relative humidity: up to 95% at 25°C
- Measuring range: 0°C to 160°C
- Accuracy: Class 1.5; stated maximum error ±2.4°C
- Switches: 4 adjustable contacts
- Switch rating: AC 220 V / 5 A
- Signals/options: Pt100; 4–20 mA and remote digital display depending on configuration
- Protection: IP55
- Selection inputs: CT secondary current, switch quantity, output, capillary length, remote display, environment code

## Oil Temperature, Oil Level, and Pressure Monitoring Device

- Temperature range: -45.0°C to 200.0°C
- Temperature accuracy: ±2°C from -45°C to 100°C; ±2% from 100°C to 200°C
- Temperature resolution: 0.1°C
- Absolute pressure: 71–181 kPa; accuracy ±2 kPa; resolution 1 kPa
- Oil-level range: 0 to -35 mm; accuracy ±2 mm; resolution 1 mm
- Ambient: -40°C to +70°C; humidity up to 95% RH at 25°C
- Atmospheric pressure: 80–110 kPa
- Protection: IP68
- Supply: DC 12–24 V; consumption ≤5 W
- Communication: RS485 to substation-area intelligent terminal
- Model reference: IB-YKTCF-OL/OP
- Standard cable: 5 m, 4-core shielded cable with bendable stainless corrugated tube

## Important terminology

- OTI: Oil Temperature Indicator, normally measuring top-oil temperature.
- WTI: Winding Temperature Indicator, normally simulating winding temperature using top-oil temperature plus load-current-related heating.
- Do not call BWY oil-temperature products “oil level thermometers” unless the product truly measures level.
- Keep “indicator,” “controller,” “thermometer,” and “monitoring device” consistent with actual functions.
